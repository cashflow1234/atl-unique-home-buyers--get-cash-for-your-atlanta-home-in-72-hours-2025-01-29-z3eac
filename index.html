About ATL Home Buyers

Founded in 2010 by Atlanta real estate veterans Mike Johnson and Sarah Chen, ATL Home Buyers began with a simple mission: provide homeowners a fast, fair way to sell their properties without the traditional hassles and delays. What started as a two-person operation has grown into Atlanta's most trusted direct home buying service, with over $100 million in successful purchases.

Our core values of transparency, integrity, and exceptional service guide everything we do. We believe every homeowner deserves a fair cash offer and stress-free selling experience, regardless of their property's condition or situation. Our team of experts works tirelessly to make the impossible possible, turning challenging property situations into simple solutions for our clients.
