Understanding Your Atlanta Home Sale Options

When it comes to selling your Atlanta home quickly, you have several options:

  • Traditional real estate listing with an agent
  • For Sale By Owner (FSBO)
  • Selling to a cash home buyer
  • Auction sales

Why Traditional Home Sales Can Take Longer

Traditional home sales in Atlanta typically take 60-90 days and involve:

  • Preparing the home for sale (repairs, staging)
  • Marketing and showing the property
  • Negotiating with potential buyers
  • Waiting for buyer financing approval
  • Home inspection and appraisal processes

Benefits of Selling to a Cash Home Buyer
